FormToCustomer

Your Marketplace for Fresh Fruits & Vegetables

Connecting Farmers and Customers for a Seamless Shopping Experience

About FormToCustomer

FormToCustomer is an innovative e-commerce platform designed to streamline the process of buying and selling fresh produce such as fruits and vegetables. The application allows farmers and vendors to list their products online, providing customers with a convenient way to shop for fresh goods from the comfort of their homes.

Key Features

  • Vendor-Seller Integration: Farmers and vendors can easily list their produce with detailed descriptions, images, and pricing.
  • User-Friendly Shopping Experience: Customers can browse fruits and vegetables, add to cart, and complete their purchase securely.
  • Order Management: Customers can track orders, while sellers manage stock and update availability in real-time.
  • Secure Payment: Multiple payment methods available, including credit/debit cards and wallets.
  • Delivery Options: Customers can choose from same-day or scheduled deliveries based on their region.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: Angular, HTML5, CSS3, TypeScript
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Authentication: Keycloak (OAuth2, OpenID Connect)
  • API Integration: REST APIs
  • Testing: Jest
  • Containerization: Docker
  • Deployment: Netlify for frontend, Custom Server Hosting for Backend
  • Version Control: Git/GitHub
